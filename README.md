# A Meatball Adventure
A Meatball Adventure is a command line game where you train a meatball to get safely escape a world of pasta to a world of freedom.
You play as a Meatball, and level up your stats across 4 regions filled with harmful enemies, which once defeated, give you experience to level up.

Features include:
Character Statistics:
  Level: Leveling up provides "Magic Mince Meat" which you can consume to enhance the following three stats
  Strength: Attack power, and decreases chance for enemy to dodge your attack
  Defense: it decreases enemy attack damage, and improves the chance for you to dodge the enemies attack
  Max HP: Increases total health your meatball can have
  
Consumables:
  Mince Meat: Heals HP
  Magic Mince Meat: As said before, consuming allows you to level up other stats
  
8 different enemies: Toothpicks, Skewers, Sauce Throwers, Spoons, Spaghetti Wranglers, Rigatoni Trappers, Forks, and The Hands
Chance based combat
Captivating Story

More Indepth game logic:
All enemies excluding the Toothpick have a chance at delivering a critical attack. This chance ranges from 15% - 30%
